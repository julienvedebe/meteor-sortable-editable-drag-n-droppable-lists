Reactive, drag and droppable, sortable and editable lists

A partially working demo, without database updates/inserts (because I don't trust the internet).

In this demo, you can move items around and rename them but it won't be reactive. You cannot add new items and the position value will not update in this version.

See the console for what's meant to happen. Alternatively clone your own copy and run it locally.
